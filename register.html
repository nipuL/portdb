Register your ports repository

You can register your personal HttpUp repository sending an email with the following information to contrib-admin at crux dot nu:

  • Repository Name (e.g. myports)
  • Root URL (e.g. http://mypage.se/ports/)
  • Your name
  • Your Email

A CRUX team member will put your repository into our database. Please give us some time to do this. Once it is active it is synced once a day. Please do not submit .httpup files, only the URL for the repository. This means the URL to the repository itself, not portspage or other indexes.
